I am a PhD student in Applied Mathematics at University of Padova under the supervision of prof. Fabio Marcuzzi, with a grant founded by BeanTech. My main scientific interests are numerical methods and algorithms for massively parallel architectures.

I graduated in 2017 at DM with a thesis focused on high-fidelity numerical simulations of incompressible unsteady Navier-Stokes equations with variable density on GPUs, in collaboration with University of Lille, where I spent a term thanks to the Erasmus+ programme. Before starting my PhD, I joined a one year research project at DEI aimed at real-time fluid flow simulations for virtual prototyping.
